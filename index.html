📢

Democratizing Skills

  • Skills Directory — live GitHub Pages site where anyone can browse, search, and discover published skills by role and use case
  • Community channel + AE newsletter digest modeled on the COO AI Digest to surface new skills, tips, and wins weekly
  • Intake workflow — automated Slack workflow captures skill requests and routes them to builders, with a Skill-Finder Skill to match people to existing skills first
  • 5-Step AI Fluency Framework — teachable methodology so anyone can go from idea to saved, reusable skill
🎯

Executive Slackbot Tour

  • 1:1 sessions with each OU leader — hands-on Slackbot walkthrough tailored to their top use cases and pain points
  • Build their first skill live — show how a prompt becomes a repeatable, automated workflow in minutes
  • Identify 2-3 high-value skills per OU that can immediately save their teams hours each week
  • Create exec champions who model AI adoption top-down and normalize Slackbot usage across their orgs
🚀

High-Impact Skill Builds

  • Structured build cadence — scoping, building, and iterating on skills in collaboration with cross-functional partners
  • Supporting staff + AE Council — co-developing skills with the people closest to the work
  • Global priority builds: NNAOV Skill, CKO Day 0 Readout, EMEA Target Account Lists, Value Deck Builder (at scale)
  • Cross-team collab with Strategy (Luyao), Marketing (Ryan Batty), and regional leads to consolidate scattered efforts
